The History of F1

The first formula 1 race was the 1946 Turin Grand Prix. The first world championship race took place at Silverstone in the England in 1950.Giuseppe Farina was the first ever world champion. F1 was originally known as Formula A.The most F1 World Championships is 7 and two drivers have gotten 7. The two drivers are Michael Schumacher and Lewis Hamilton.
