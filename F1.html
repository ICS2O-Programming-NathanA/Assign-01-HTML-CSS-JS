Formula 1



Formula 1 is a motosport. Formula 1 races take place on weekends and it goes throughout the whole weekend. On the Friday of the race weekend the drivers do 2 free practises. They can test out the track and determine if they need to make ajustements. They have 90 minutes for all the free practises. On Saturday morning they have another free practise. Then they have qualifiers. This determines where they will start on the grid. Then on Sunday they have the race. There are currently 20 drivers and 10 teams in F1.The Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA) determines the rules and regulations. There are races held everywhere in the world.
